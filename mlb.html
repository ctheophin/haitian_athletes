Touki Touissant

A pitcher for the Atlanta Braves.

Stats Bio Highlights

Touki-Touissant is a professional baseball player. He's a pitcher for the Atlanta Braves. He was born on June 20th, 1996 in Pembroke Pines, Florida, his father is Haitian and his mother is Kenyan. He moved to Haiti as an infant and came back to Florida when he was 6. He was selected 16th overall in the 2014 MLB Draft by the Arizona Diamondbacks. Check out his highlights!
